COUNTRIES THAT FEED THE WORLD

Food is a fundamental economic product, but only a handful of countries actually excel at food production. Most agricultural commodities require a lot of land, which only the largest countries have in abundance. According to Food and Agriculture Organization's data, China, India, United States, Brazil and Russian have long been considered the top food suppliers in the world.

China always out-produces the U.S., and in some years India produces more food than the U.S.
